Gem - Privacy Policy

Updated 2022 December 18

Processed Data

Photos and text

Gem accesses only the photos you select using the iOS system photo picker.

Your photos are stored on device. If the system backup is enabled, the photos and text are stored by Apple on iCloud. More info on the backup on Apple's website: https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/icloud/mm122d3ef202/icloud

Third party services

Use of RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to help process the in-app payments in the app. We do not send any of your information to RevenueCat, but they may collect information via their SDK. You can view RevenueCat's privacy policy here: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy, and more information on the data they may collect here: https://docs.revenuecat.com/docs/apple-app-privacy.
